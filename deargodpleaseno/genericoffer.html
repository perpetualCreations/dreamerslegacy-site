Description

Position for developing, maintaining, and testing computer hardware and software. You will collaborate in teams and work alongside developers, engineers, and IT professionals, on projects.

Part of your work will also include quality-assurance of products, and perform testing as needed. The results from these tests will be passed onto revising designs.

Some examples of your day-to-day activities include programming and designing hardware solutions and electronic circuits.

Prerequisites

Applicants should have a solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, mathematics, graphics, statistics, and data visuialization.

The minimum education required is a bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, programming, or IT.

Your application will be more visible with a higher education such as a major. Senior positions are recommended to have a masters.

A certification is recommended.

Previous experience in this field, whether amateur or professional, will greatly assist consideration of your application and your adjustment into the workplace.

This includes learning to use tools such as Integrated Development Environments (referred to as IDEs) and version control software.

About

This position is responsible for developing the daily software applications you use, and the hardware it runs on.

You may take these luxuries for granted, for your day-to-day productivity and conveniences such as ordering online and digital assistants.

Many common workflows across a range of industries make use of computer applications and computerized systems, such as CAD software used by many disciplines of engineers.
